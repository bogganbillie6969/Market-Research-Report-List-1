Adhesive shielding tapes feature a conductive adhesive backing, allowing for easy application on various surfaces to block electromagnetic interference (EMI). In contrast, non-adhesive shielding tapes rely on mechanical fastening or wrapping techniques, providing flexibility in use without residue. Both types serve critical roles in protecting electronic components, ensuring signal integrity, and enhancing the performance of devices in diverse applications.</p></p>
