Enzyme-based formulations aid in debridement and wound healing by breaking down necrotic tissue. Growth factors, essential proteins that promote cell proliferation and repair, enhance the healing process by stimulating tissue regeneration. Together, these advanced technologies optimize wound management and improve patient outcomes in complex wounds.</p></p>
