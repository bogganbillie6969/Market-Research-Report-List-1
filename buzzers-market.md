<p><strong>The Buzzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Buzzers are categorized primarily into two market types: electromagnetic and piezoelectric. Electromagnetic buzzers operate using an electromagnet to generate sound when an electric current passes through. They are typically larger and offer lower frequencies, making them suitable for alarm systems and large appliances. In contrast, piezoelectric buzzers utilize the piezoelectric effect to produce sound by vibrating a diaphragm, allowing for compact designs and higher frequencies. This makes them ideal for handheld devices and compact electronics. Both types serve diverse applications in varying industries.</p></p>
