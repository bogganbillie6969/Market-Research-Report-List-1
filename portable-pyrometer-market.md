<p><p>The Portable Pyrometer Market is primarily categorized into two types: Optical Technology and Infrared Technology. Optical pyrometers measure temperature by detecting the visible light emitted from a hot object, providing accurate readings for high-temperature applications. In contrast, Infrared pyrometers utilize infrared radiation to gauge temperature without direct contact, making them ideal for diverse industrial settings. Both technologies cater to varying temperature ranges and environmental conditions, highlighting their significance in quality control and process monitoring across multiple industries.</p></p>
