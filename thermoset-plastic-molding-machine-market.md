<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoset plastic molding machines are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their orientation and operation. Vertical machines have a compact design, with the mold moving up and down, ideal for insert molding and smaller parts. Horizontal machines feature a side-to-side mold movement, suitable for high-volume production and larger components. Both types cater to industries like automotive, electronics, and construction, offering precision and efficiency in molding thermoset plastics, which harden permanently when heated.</p></p>
