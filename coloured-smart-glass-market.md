<p><strong>The Coloured Smart Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic Smart Glass</li><li>SPD Smart Glass</li><li>PDLC Smart Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The colored smart glass market comprises three primary types: Electrochromic Smart Glass, which changes tint in response to electrical currents, allowing for dynamic light control; SPD (Suspended Particle Device) Smart Glass, which offers rapid tint adjustment by manipulating suspended particles, providing instant privacy and glare reduction; and PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) Smart Glass, which shifts from opaque to transparent when an electric current is applied, ensuring versatility in both privacy and aesthetics. Each technology caters to distinct architectural and automotive needs.</p></p>
