<p><p>Pulse Width Modulation (PWM) controllers are essential in managing power delivery in electronic circuits. The market is primarily divided into two types: Current Mode PWM Controllers and Voltage Mode PWM Controllers. Current Mode PWM Controllers regulate output by monitoring the inductor current, providing improved response to load changes and enhanced stability. In contrast, Voltage Mode PWM Controllers focus on maintaining a constant output voltage by adjusting the duty cycle, ensuring effective power conversion but with potential limitations in transient response. Both serve distinct applications across various industries.</p></p>
