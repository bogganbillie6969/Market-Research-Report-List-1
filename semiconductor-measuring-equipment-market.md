<p><ul><li>Semiconductor Measuring Front-end Equipment</li><li>Semiconductor Measuring Back-end Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor measuring equipment market is divided into two main types: front-end equipment and back-end equipment. Front-end equipment focuses on processes that occur during wafer fabrication, including measurement of parameters like thickness, layer composition, and electrical properties essential for quality control. Back-end equipment, on the other hand, is involved in packaging and assembly processes, ensuring the final products meet specified performance metrics. Both segments are vital for maintaining efficiency, precision, and reliability in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
