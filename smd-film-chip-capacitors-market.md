<p><p>SMD film chip capacitors are categorized based on the dielectric materials used in their construction. PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide) film capacitors offer high thermal stability, making them suitable for harsh environments. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) film capacitors are valued for their versatility and cost-effectiveness, widely used in electronic circuits. PEN (Polyethylene Naphthalate) film capacitors provide superior electrical performance and stability over a broad temperature range. Other types may include various specialized films, each tailored for specific applications or performance characteristics.</p></p>
