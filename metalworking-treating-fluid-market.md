<p><p>The metalworking treating fluid market comprises semi-synthetic and synthetic fluids, each offering unique benefits. Semi-synthetic fluids are blends of mineral oil and synthetic components, providing a balanced mix of lubrication, cooling, and corrosion protection, making them suitable for various machining operations. Synthetic fluids, on the other hand, are entirely chemical-based, offering superior performance in extreme conditions, excellent cooling properties, and reduced environmental impact. Both types are essential for improving efficiency, productivity, and the longevity of equipment in metalworking processes.</p></p>
