<p><ul><li>Optical System</li><li>Electromagnetic System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cranial navigation system market encompasses two primary types: optical systems and electromagnetic systems. Optical systems utilize cameras and tracking devices to provide real-time visualization and navigation during surgical procedures, offering high precision and accuracy. In contrast, electromagnetic systems rely on magnetic fields to track instrument positions, allowing for flexible navigation without line-of-sight limitations. Both systems enhance surgical outcomes by improving accuracy and reducing risks, making them essential tools in neurosurgery and related fields.</p></p>
