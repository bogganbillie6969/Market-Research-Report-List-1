<p><strong>The Profilometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Profilometer</li><li>Non-contact Profilometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The profilometer market is segmented into contact and non-contact types. Contact profilometers utilize stylus probes to physically trace the surface, offering high accuracy and resolution for roughness measurement. Conversely, non-contact profilometers employ optical methods, such as laser or blue light, to capture surface profiles without physical interaction, making them suitable for delicate or soft materials. Both types cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing and materials science, providing critical data for quality control and surface characterization.</p></p>
