<p><p>The empty capsules market is primarily divided into two main types: gelatin capsules and non-gelatin capsules. Gelatin capsules, made from animal-derived gelatin, are widely used due to their excellent barrier properties and ease of digestion. Non-gelatin capsules, often made from plant-derived materials such as hydroxypropyl methylcellulose (HPMC), cater to vegetarian and vegan preferences, offering flexibility in formulations. Both types serve various applications across pharmaceuticals, nutraceuticals, and dietary supplements, addressing diverse consumer demands for health and wellness products.</p></p>
