<p><strong>The Ampoule Leak Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Decay Detection Technology</li><li>Pressure Decay Detection Technology</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ampoule leak testing machine market encompasses various technologies to ensure product integrity. Vacuum decay detection technology measures changes in pressure within an ampoule, identifying leaks by monitoring how quickly the vacuum pressure stabilizes. Pressure decay detection technology, on the other hand, applies pressure and observes any drop, indicating a potential leak. Other methods may include tracer gas or bubble tests, each offering specific advantages depending on the application, such as sensitivity and speed, catering to diverse manufacturing needs.</p></p>
