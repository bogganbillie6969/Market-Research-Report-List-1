<p><ul><li>Horizontal Axis Wind Turbine</li><li>Vertical Axis Wind Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small wind turbine market primarily comprises two types: horizontal axis wind turbines (HAWT) and vertical axis wind turbines (VAWT). HAWTs, featuring blades positioned horizontally, are typically more efficient and suited for areas with consistent wind patterns. In contrast, VAWTs have a vertical design, allowing them to capture wind from any direction, making them ideal for turbulent environments. Both types cater to residential, agricultural, and small commercial applications, promoting renewable energy adoption and reducing electricity costs for users.</p></p>
