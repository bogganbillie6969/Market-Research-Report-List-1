<p><strong>The Agricultural Centrifugal Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage Pump</li><li>Multi-stage Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agricultural centrifugal pumps are categorized into single-stage and multi-stage pumps based on their design and application. Single-stage pumps consist of a single impeller and are ideal for low to medium lift applications, efficiently moving water over shorter distances. In contrast, multi-stage pumps contain multiple impellers, allowing them to generate higher pressure and lift water over greater distances, making them suitable for large-scale irrigation or deep well applications. Both types are vital for efficient agricultural water management.</p></p>
