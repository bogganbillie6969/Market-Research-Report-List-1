<p><p>The aliphatic amine market comprises three main types: primary, secondary, and tertiary aliphatic amines. Primary aliphatic amines have one alkyl or aryl group attached to the nitrogen atom, making them highly reactive and versatile in applications. Secondary aliphatic amines feature two substituents on the nitrogen, enhancing stability and offering distinct chemical properties. Tertiary aliphatic amines possess three alkyl or aryl groups attached to nitrogen, which further influences their solubility and reactivity, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
