<p><p>The MEMS optical mirrors market is primarily divided into two types: 1-axis scan mirrors and 2-axis scan mirrors. 1-axis scan mirrors are designed for applications requiring movement along a single plane, often used in basic laser scanning and barcode readers. In contrast, 2-axis scan mirrors offer dual-plane movement, allowing for more complex scanning applications such as 3D imaging, laser projectors, and advanced LiDAR systems. Both types are essential in enhancing optical systems' precision and versatility across various industries.</p></p>
