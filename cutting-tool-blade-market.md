<p><ul><li>Carbide</li><li>CBN</li><li>Ceramic</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cutting tool blade market is categorized primarily into four types: carbide, CBN (cubic boron nitride), ceramic, and other materials. Carbide blades are renowned for their hardness and wear resistance, making them suitable for various machining processes. CBN blades excel in cutting hard materials and offer exceptional thermal stability. Ceramic blades provide high-speed machining capabilities with excellent wear resistance but are more brittle. Other materials include high-speed steel and composites, catering to niche applications and specific machining needs.</p></p>
