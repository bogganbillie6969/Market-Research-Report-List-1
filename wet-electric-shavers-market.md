<p><p>The wet electric shavers market is primarily divided into two types: rotary and foil. Rotary shavers use circular blades that move in a circular motion, making them ideal for navigating contours and providing a smooth shave for those with thicker or longer hair. Foil shavers, on the other hand, feature oscillating blades under a thin layer of foil, offering a closer shave and better suited for fine hair. Each type caters to different user preferences and skin sensitivities, enhancing shaving experiences.</p></p>
