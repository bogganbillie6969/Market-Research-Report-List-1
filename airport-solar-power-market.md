<p><p>The airport solar power market encompasses various photovoltaic (PV) technologies. Silicon PV is the most common, known for its efficiency and durability. Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) PV offers high efficiency and performance in limited space but at a higher cost. Cadmium Telluride (CdTe) PV is cost-effective and suitable for large installations but less efficient than silicon. CIS/CIGS PV combines flexibility and lightweight features, ideal for diverse airport architectures, though still developing in terms of market share and efficiency.</p></p>
