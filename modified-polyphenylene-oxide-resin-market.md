<p><ul><li>Multiphase Mixing PPO Resin</li><li>Two-phase Mixing PPO Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Modified Polyphenylene Oxide (MPPO) Resin market includes two primary types: Multiphase Mixing PPO Resin and Two-phase Mixing PPO Resin. Multiphase Mixing PPO Resin involves the combination of PPO with other materials to enhance properties like toughness and impact resistance. In contrast, Two-phase Mixing PPO Resin focuses on blending PPO with compatibilizers to achieve better dispersion and mechanical performance. Both types cater to diverse applications, including automotive, electronics, and construction, due to their enhanced thermal and chemical stability.</p></p>
