<p><p>Ultra High Pressure Processing (HPP) is a food preservation technique that utilizes high pressure to extend shelf life while maintaining the nutritional quality and safety of food products. This process involves subjecting food to pressures up to 600 MPa, which inactivates pathogens and spoilage organisms without the need for high temperatures. As a result, HPP foods retain their fresh taste, color, and nutritional value, making them increasingly appealing to health-conscious consumers.</p><p>The Ultra High Pressure Processing HPP Food Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by rising consumer demand for minimally processed, safe, and healthy food products.
