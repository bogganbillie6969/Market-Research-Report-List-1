<p><p>The medical plastic products market includes various types of materials, each serving specific applications. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is widely used for medical tubing and blood bags due to its durability. PP (Polypropylene) is favored for rigid containers and surgical equipment because of its sterilization properties. PE (Polyethylene) is common in disposable items and packaging solutions for its lightweight and flexibility. PS (Polystyrene) is often used for labware and diagnostic devices due to its clarity and ease of manufacturing. Other materials encompass alternatives like PET and thermoplastic elastomers, catering to specialized medical needs.</p></p>
