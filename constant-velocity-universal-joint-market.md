<p><strong>The Constant Velocity Universal Joint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball</li><li>Fork</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Constant Velocity Universal Joint (CV Joint) market is primarily segmented into Ball and Fork types. Ball joints utilize spherical balls to transmit torque and accommodate angular misalignments, offering smooth motion and durability, commonly found in front-wheel-drive vehicles. Fork joints, on the other hand, use a fork-like structure to connect shafts, providing a robust solution for high-torque applications, often seen in rear-wheel-drive systems. Both types are essential for enhancing drivetrain efficiency and vehicle handling across automotive platforms.</p></p>
