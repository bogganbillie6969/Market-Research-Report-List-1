<p><p>The polycarbonate (PC) resin market is primarily categorized into two types: phosgene type and non-phosgene type. Phosgene-type polycarbonate is synthesized using phosgene gas, a process that offers high efficiency and quality, making it popular in industrial applications. In contrast, non-phosgene type polycarbonate is produced through alternative methods that bypass phosgene, often focusing on sustainability and safety. Non-phosgene methods are gaining traction due to their reduced environmental impact, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers and industries.</p></p>
