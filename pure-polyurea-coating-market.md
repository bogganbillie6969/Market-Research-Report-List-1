<p><p>The Pure Polyurea Coating Market is categorized into two main types: aliphatic and aromatic polyurea coatings. Aliphatic polyurea coatings are known for their UV stability and color retention, making them ideal for outdoor applications. They provide durable, glossy finishes with excellent weather resistance. On the other hand, aromatic polyurea coatings are characterized by their rapid curing properties and high tensile strength, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. They are typically more cost-effective but may fade when exposed to sunlight.</p></p>
