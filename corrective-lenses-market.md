<p><strong>The Corrective Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Divergent Lens</li><li>Convergent Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrective lenses market is primarily divided into two main types: divergent lenses and convergent lenses. Divergent lenses, also known as concave lenses, are used to correct nearsightedness by spreading light rays outward, allowing distant objects to be seen clearly. In contrast, convergent lenses, or convex lenses, help correct farsightedness by bending light rays inward, enabling the eye to focus on nearby objects. Both types cater to distinct vision correction needs, driving demand in the optical industry.</p></p>
