<p><p>The automotive generator market is divided into AC and DC types. AC generators, also known as alternators, produce alternating current and are commonly used in modern vehicles due to their efficiency, compact design, and ability to charge batteries effectively. DC generators, though less common today, produce direct current and were traditionally used in older vehicles. Both types convert mechanical energy into electrical energy, but AC generators dominate the market due to their superior performance and compatibility with modern automotive systems.</p></p>
