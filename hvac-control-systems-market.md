<p><p>The HVAC control systems market comprises three primary segments: sensors, controlled devices, and controllers. Sensors monitor environmental conditions such as temperature, humidity, and air quality, providing critical data for system adjustments. Controlled devices, including dampers, valves, and fans, act on the controller’s commands to regulate airflow and temperature effectively. Controllers are the brain of the system, processing sensor inputs to maintain desired environmental settings, optimize energy usage, and enhance overall system performance for comfort and efficiency.</p></p>
