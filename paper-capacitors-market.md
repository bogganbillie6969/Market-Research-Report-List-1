<p><strong>The Paper Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paper Sheet Capacitors</li><li>Metalized Paper Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper capacitors market is primarily categorized into two types: paper sheet capacitors and metalized paper capacitors. Paper sheet capacitors consist of layers of paper insulation and conductive plates, offering high capacitance and good frequency characteristics for various applications. In contrast, metalized paper capacitors feature a thin layer of metal vapor-deposited onto the paper, enhancing their efficiency and reducing size while maintaining performance. Both types are widely used in electronics, providing reliable energy storage and filtering capabilities.</p></p>
