<p><p>Automotive LED headlights are advanced lighting systems that enhance visibility and safety. Adaptive Front Lighting (AFL) headlights adjust beam direction based on steering input and vehicle speed, improving illumination around curves. Adaptive Driving Beam (ADB) headlights dynamically control light distribution, selectively dimming areas to avoid glare for oncoming traffic while maintaining optimal brightness elsewhere. Both technologies offer superior performance, energy efficiency, and customization, catering to the growing demand for smart, adaptive lighting solutions in modern vehicles.</p></p>
