<p><ul><li>Dilution Nitrogen Purging</li><li>Displacement Nitrogen Purging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nitrogen Purging System market comprises two main types: Dilution Nitrogen Purging and Displacement Nitrogen Purging. Dilution Nitrogen Purging involves introducing nitrogen to reduce the concentration of harmful gases or contaminants in a space, effectively diluting the impurities. In contrast, Displacement Nitrogen Purging involves replacing the existing gas in a system with nitrogen, ensuring that the unwanted gases are expelled. Both methods are essential for enhancing safety and efficiency in various industrial applications, particularly in chemical processing and storage.</p></p>
