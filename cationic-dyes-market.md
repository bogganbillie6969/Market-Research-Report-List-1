<p><ul><li>Isolated Type</li><li>Conjugate Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cationic dyes market is categorized into two main types: isolated type and conjugate type. Isolated type cationic dyes are characterized by their individual dye molecules, which provide vibrant colors and high affinity for fibers, especially synthetics. In contrast, conjugate type cationic dyes comprise a combination of dye and other chemical structures, enhancing their stability and lightfastness. Both types serve distinct applications in textiles, paper, and other industries, depending on the required performance characteristics and desired aesthetic effects.</p></p>
