<p><ul><li>Dry Core Treatment</li><li>Dry Core and Sheath Treatment</li><li>Dry Sheath Treatment</li><li>Non-dry Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The climbing ropes market features several treatment types that enhance performance in various conditions. **Dry Core Treatment** involves applying water-repellent treatments to the rope's core, offering moisture resistance without affecting the sheath. **Dry Core and Sheath Treatment** ensures both the inner and outer layers repel water, maximizing protection against wet environments. **Dry Sheath Treatment** focuses solely on treating the outer sheath, improving durability and grip. **Non-dry Treatment** lacks moisture-resistant properties, making it suitable for dry conditions only, but often is more affordable.</p></p>
